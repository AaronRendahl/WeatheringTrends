fits <- FitElementRatios(c("Sr", "Pb", "CaO"), c("Zr", "V"), "depth.top", welldata, profile=FALSE, verbose=FALSE)
plot(fits)

Testing this out

Can also change the fit so its linear on the log(ratio) scale instead of on the ratio scale.

Original method, linear on the ratio scale.

(fita <- FitElementRatio("Sr", "Zr", "depth.top", welldata, profile=FALSE))

## Fit of Sr/Zr
##

par(mfrow=c(1,2))

Computing tau directly

Can either take the average on the ratio scale or on the logratio scale. Plotting is done directly, so far.

library(WeatheringTrends)
data(welldata)
foo1 <- FitTau("Sr", "Zr", "depth.top", welldata, cutoff=7.5)
## fitting Sr/Zr
with(foo1$data, {
  plot(tau, -depth, main="tau")
  abline(h=-foo1$cutoff, v=0)
})
